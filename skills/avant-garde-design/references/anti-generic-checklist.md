# Anti-Generic Design Checklist

## Forbidden Patterns (The "Safe Harbor")

| Pattern | Why Avoid | Think Instead |
|---------|-----------|---------------|
| **Bento grids** | Modern cliché, every AI design | Why does this content NEED a grid? |
| **Hero split (left/right)** | Predictable & boring | Massive typography or vertical narrative |
| **Mesh/Aurora gradients** | Lazy "floating blobs" background | Radical color pairing |
| **Glassmorphism (blue/white)** | AI's idea of "premium" | Solid, high-contrast flat |
| **Deep Cyan / Fintech Blue** | Safe harbor from purple ban | Red, Black, Neon Green |
| **"Orchestrate / Empower"** | AI-generated copywriting | How would a human say this? |
| **Dark + neon glow** | Overused "AI look" | What does BRAND actually need? |
| **Rounded everything** | Generic/safe | Where can I use sharp, brutalist edges? |
| **Inter/Roboto defaults** | Without typographical hierarchy | Distinctive type pairings |
| **Purple-gradient-on-white** | Cliché | Unexpected combinations |

> **"Every 'safe' structure brings you closer to a generic template. TAKE RISKS."**

---

## Complete Anti-Generic Checklist

Before any design decision, run through all items:

### Strategic Alignment

- [ ] Does every element have a defensible "why"?
- [ ] Is typography intentional, not default?
- [ ] Does color serve function, not just decoration?
- [ ] Is whitespace structural, not accidental?
- [ ] Are we showing or telling the brand promise?

### Design Quality

- [ ] Would this work without color?
- [ ] Is animation purposeful or decorative?
- [ ] Does this match audience psychology?
- [ ] Are we copying patterns or adapting principles?
- [ ] Would this feel dated in 3 years?
- [ ] Does this avoid "AI slop" aesthetics?
- [ ] Is this distinguishable from template designs?
- [ ] Have we rejected safe defaults intentionally?
- [ ] Does this align with Strategic Positioning quadrant?

### Technical Excellence

- [ ] Library primitives used (not rebuilt)?
- [ ] Accessibility engineered from start?
- [ ] Performance budget respected?
- [ ] `prefers-reduced-motion` honored?

## Universal Truths

### Truth 1: Intentionality is the Only Differentiator

> iTrust is not "boring." It is intentionally restrained. Kimi is not "flashy." It is intentionally bold. The worst state is an unintentional design.

**Application:** Document "why" for every major decision. If you cannot articulate it, the decision is not yet intentional.

### Truth 2: Hierarchy is a Sacred Duty

> Both sites have impeccable hierarchy. User never wonders what to look at next.

**Application:** Test by squinting. If hierarchy collapses, redesign.

### Truth 3: Whitespace is a Voice, Not an Absence

> In iTrust, whitespace speaks organization and calm. In Kimi, it speaks premium isolation and drama.

**Application:** Before adding any element, ask: "Could whitespace achieve this goal instead?"

### Truth 4: Accessibility is a Mark of Mastery

> Both demonstrate that great designers engineer for inclusion. Neither uses complexity as an excuse for exclusion.

**Application:** Accessibility is not a constraint—it is a design material. Engineer for it from the start.

## Design Prompt Library

### On First Principles

| Prompt | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| "If our brand were a person, would they be a tenured professor or a startup founder?" | Sets tone |
| "Does our user need a handshake or a high-five?" | Handshake=trust, High-five=energy |
| "Are we selling a safe harbor or a rocket ship?" | Safe harbor=stability, Rocket ship=transformation |

### On Typography

| Prompt | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| "If our headlines were spoken, would they be whispered or shouted?" | Whisper=refined, Shout=bold |
| "Is our typeface a workhorse or a show pony?" | Workhorse=utility, Show pony=distinctive |
| "What would our typography look like without any words?" | Focus on scale, weight, texture |

### On Color

| Prompt | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| "If our brand had a flavor, would it be mineral water or an energy drink?" | Mineral water=clean, Energy drink=intense |
| "Does our palette make the user feel calm or courageous?" | Calm=low saturation, Courageous=high saturation |
| "If we removed all color, would our design still communicate brand?" | Tests color as crutch |

### On Layout

| Prompt | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| "Does our grid feel like a comfortable home or an exciting city?" | Home=predictable, City=dynamic |
| "If our page were a room, would furniture be against walls or floating?" | Against walls=traditional, Floating=modern |
| "Where do we want the user's eye to struggle?" | Creates intentional visual tension |

### On Imagery & Interaction

| Prompt | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| "Do we show what they will become, or how they will feel?" | Result vs. emotion |
| "If our site made a sound, would it be a library or a concert hall?" | Library=quiet, Concert=energetic |
| "Does our animation serve the story or just decorate?" | If decoration, delete it |
